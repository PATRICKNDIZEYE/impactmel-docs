## Frequently asked questions

### Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade at any time and your billing is prorated. Downgrading takes effect at the next renewal date.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes. Every new organization gets a 30-day free trial with full Growth plan features, no credit card required. After the trial, choose a plan or stay on the free tier (1 program, 5 indicators, 5 team members).

### What counts as a "team member"?

Anyone with a login: admins, M&E officers, reporters, and viewers. External donors who receive a shared report link do not count — they never log in.

### We are a network of partner organizations. How does pricing work?

Each organization manages its own subscription. If you are running a hub-and-spoke model with sub-grantees, the Enterprise sub-grantee portal lets partner organizations submit data into your workspace without needing their own subscription.

### We receive institutional funding and need to budget annually. Do you offer annual pricing?

Yes. Annual billing is available at a 20% discount on Growth and Enterprise plans. Contact **sales@impactmel.com** for a quote.

### Can we self-host?

Self-hosted deployment is available for Enterprise customers with data sovereignty requirements. Contact us to discuss your infrastructure needs.

### Is donor data kept separate from other organizations?

Yes. Every organization is a fully isolated tenant. Your data — programs, indicators, reports, beneficiary data — is never visible to other organizations or accessible to ImpactMEL staff without your explicit request.

## Non-profit pricing

ImpactMEL is built for the development sector. We offer a **40% discount** for registered non-profit organizations on all plans. Send proof of registration (NGO certificate, 501(c)(3) determination letter, or equivalent) to **billing@impactmel.com**.
